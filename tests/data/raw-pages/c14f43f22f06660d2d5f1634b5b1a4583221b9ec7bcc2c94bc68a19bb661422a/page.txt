== Regional Styles? ==

There are a few other regional styles that could be listed, but I'm not sure if this is going too much into details.

Chichibu yatai bayashi is another regional style that could be listed. Bender talks about this on page 73.

Additionally, Oedo Sukeroku is often credited as its own style with its use of naname slant stands. Much of North American taiko is rooted in the style of Oedo Sukeroku. Bender refers to this on page 192. [[Special:Contributions/24.63.251.4|24.63.251.4]] ([[User talk:24.63.251.4|talk]]) 22:29, 8 July 2023 (UTC)
