== New topic structure ==

Hello all - I see that there is a plan to split the article into 2d and 3d animation pages but I have a different idea - given that animation has advanced so much, there is now 1d, 2d, 3d, 4d (yes it exists now), and hybrid. So instead I would like to remove the 2 parts and discuss the coordinate systems and how they make the animation disciplines different. This will also enable us to make the article more specific on the topics which are ubiquitous across, for example, 2d and 3d animation.
