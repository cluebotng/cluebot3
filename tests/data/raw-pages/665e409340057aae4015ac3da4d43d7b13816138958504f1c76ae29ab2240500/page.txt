== Article rewrite ==

For anyone who is interested, I am going to attempt to rewrite the article [[User:Epicgenius/sandbox/article-draft3|in my sandbox]], with the goal of getting it back to Good Article status. Although I haven't added much to the draft yet, I plan to rewrite large parts of the page from scratch. 

Currently, the article is disorganized, giving undue weight to certain aspects of the fair (e.g. some pavilions have three paragraphs, while other pavilions have a single sentence or even nothing at all). There is a lot that can be said about other aspects, such as food. There are a lot of sourcing issues in the page, including info sourced to blogs and other unreliable sources. It might take me several months to rewrite this page, but I'm hoping to iron out all of the issues that currently exist. &ndash; [[User:Epicgenius|Epicgenius]] ([[User talk:Epicgenius|talk]]) 21:49, 20 May 2024 (UTC)
