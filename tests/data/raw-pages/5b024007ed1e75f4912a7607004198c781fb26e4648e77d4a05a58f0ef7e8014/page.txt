== Grammatical number of "ZZ Top" ==

''ZZ Top has released 15 studio albums and sold an estimated 50 million records worldwide. They have won three MTV Video Music Awards''

I believe, or at least I have seen it mentioned, that, in AmE, bands are treated as grammatically singular; so, whereas in BrE we would say "ZZ Top have released", in AmE it is "has released". Even so, is it not illogical, or disruptive to reading, to follow "ZZ Top has" with "They have"?
