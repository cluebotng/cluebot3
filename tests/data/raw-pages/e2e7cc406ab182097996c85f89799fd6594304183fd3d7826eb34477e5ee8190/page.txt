== Neutral Point of View ==

I am no expert in this field nor pretend to be. But I have done a lot of research into Paleontology and the human response - i.e. domestication, bronze age, Iron Age, etc... Most articles I read on Wikipedia are for the most part scientific and consider both sides in a neutral point of view as all scientific work should be.  That being said, I have never read an article (outside of politics) that is so one-sided in the presented view.  Nothing in science is that one-sided. There is little debate and what is the small amount included is quickly dismissed.  The wording itself is many times biased. I think this article could benefit from being more objective.
