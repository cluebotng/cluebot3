== White Base merge ==

I think [[White Base]] should be merged with the general article about ''MS Gundam''. Though the White Base article has some merits and relevant analyses, thanks to its "Development" section, I don't think the vehicle itself has demonstrated notability outside of affilitated sources, as it only gets trivial or inconsequential mentions (such as being featured in an obscure listing). The details on the WB creation are interesting, but generally fit the overall themes of mecha design in ''MS Gundam'', and of the conflict between Tomino and sponsors. All these comments (which are not too long) should be included here because they'd give more substance to [[Mobile_Suit_Gundam#Original concept and development]], which would help the article reach GA status. The rest of the WB article being plot summary, it could easily be used to flesh out [[Mobile_Suit_Gundam#Plot]] if necessary.
